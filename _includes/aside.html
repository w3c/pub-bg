Group Communications

Mailing lists

  • public-publishingbg@w3.org: the group’s primary mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are publicly visible, and anyone can post a mail.
  • internal-publishingbg@w3.org: the group’s member confidential mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are visible to W3C members and Business Group members only, and only group members can post a mail.
  • public-publishing-sc@w3.org: mailing list of the group’s Steering Committee. The archives are publicly visible, and anybody can post a mail.
